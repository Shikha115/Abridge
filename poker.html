poker stars india

brand: Poker Stars India

Celebrity: Nawazuddin Siddiqui

Services Provided: Endorsement, Social Media

Description:
Endorsement: We worked with Poker Stars India to understand the market, calibrate their brand strategy and to identify the right face for the brand in India. With names like Ronaldo, Messi, Neymar, Federer, Kevin Hart, Andrew Flintoff as their global brand ambassadors, we were looking at a celebrity who could break the clutter and make an impact. With Sacred Games making Ganesh Gaitonde an icon and his reach down south with Petta alongside Rajnikanth, Nawazuddin Siddiqui made an ideal fit given his mass appeal.

Social Media: For Poker Stars India social media campaign before the launch of Nawazuddin Siddiqui's TV campaign, we worked with popular digital influencers Kusha Kapila and Varun Thakur to create engaging Poker related content for Instagram.
