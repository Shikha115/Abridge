Flipkart

brand: Flipkart

Celebrity: Prosenjit Chatterjee, Mahesh Manjrekar, Raghav Juyyal, Dharmesh & Neha Dhupia

Services Provided: Endorsement, Social Media

Description:
Endorsement: For the Flipkart Big Billion Day Sale 2018 campaign, we signed on influential celebrities from different regions of India to endorse the various product categories available on Flipkart. The celebrities we collaborated with were - Prosenjit Chatterjee, the biggest Superstar from the Kolkata film industry, Mahesh Manjrekar the big boss of Marathi Cinema and Raghav Juyyal and Dharmesh who have a big reach in Indian households through the TV show 'Dance India Dance'.

Social Media: For Flipkart's Gen E campaign, we brought on board the very popular Indian celebrity and mom Neha Dhupia to share her views about parenting and raising children who belong to the Generation "E'. Neha Dhupia posted this content on her Facebook handle.
