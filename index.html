ABOUT US

Abridge is a boutique agency that caters to end to end talent and content driven marketing solutions for brands. Our services vary from influencer engagements to content creation. With in depth market knowledge and industry relations, Abridge places itself as an expert talent & content agency dedicated to bridging the gap between brands and entertainment.

Providing tactical solutions is our forte. We believe in creating and maintaining long term relationships with all the brands and agencies that we work with by providing impactful influencer marketing solutions through a shared vision of a high Return on Investment for our brands.
